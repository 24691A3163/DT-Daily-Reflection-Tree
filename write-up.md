# Design Rationale

## Approach

I designed this system as a deterministic reflection tool based on three psychological axes: locus of control, contribution vs entitlement, and radius of concern.

## Question Design

The questions are designed to feel natural and realistic. Each option represents a genuine human response instead of an ideal answer, encouraging honest reflection.

## Branching Logic

The tree branches based on user responses, ensuring that each path leads to a specific reflection. Internal vs external thinking leads to different insights.

## Psychological Foundations

* Locus of Control (Rotter)
* Growth Mindset (Dweck)
* Organizational Citizenship Behavior
* Self-Transcendence (Maslow)

## Trade-offs

To maintain determinism, I restricted all inputs to fixed options. This reduces flexibility but ensures consistency and reliability.

## Improvements

With more time, I would expand the tree depth, improve summaries, and build a simple UI for better interaction.
